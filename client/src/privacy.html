Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

The Beynum Company ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Virtual Team Parent application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy is to inform you of our policies regarding the access, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Our commitment to you is simple: We respect your privacy. We have designed our Service to be a tool that *you* control, operating with your explicit permission.

Our Data Policy: Limited Use with Your Permission

To function, the Virtual Team Parent service requires your explicit permission to access certain Google services associated with your account (such as Gmail and Google Docs/Drive). This access is *only* used to manage team communications on your behalf.

We do not collect your personal data for our own use. We do not sell, distribute, or share your data with any third parties. Our Service acts as a secure tool to perform tasks you initiate.

Google Services Access

With your permission, our Service may access the following Google services for the sole purposes listed:

  • Google Gmail API: To send emails (such as blast communications and weekly updates) *from your account* to the parents and players on your team. We do not read, store, or delete your other emails.
  • Google Drive / Docs API: To access specific documents (such as team rosters or highlight notes) that you explicitly give the script access to. This allows the Service to pull information for team updates and insights. We do not access, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.

Data Sharing and Distribution

Because we do not collect your data for our own purposes, we have no data to sell, share, or distribute. Any data accessed (e.g., from a Google Doc) is only used to perform the requested action (e.g., send an email to your team) and is not stored by us.

Data Storage

Our Service operates without storing your personal data on our servers. We do not save copies of the emails you send or the content of the documents we access. Any information required for the app to function (such as your authorization token for Google or team contact lists you provide) is handled securely and is not used for any purpose other than operating the Service.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is a tool intended for managing administrative communication **between adults** (coaches and parents/guardians).

The Service is not intended for, and must not be used for, communicating any information or data about children or students on the team. This includes, but is not limited to, student names, performance, grades, health information, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) related to a minor.

We do not knowingly collect, process, or store any PII related to children. The User (coach, school, etc.) is responsible for ensuring that all content processed by the Service complies with this policy, their school's policies, and all applicable laws, including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act).

Any use of the Service to transmit such information is considered misuse and a direct violation of our Terms of Service.
